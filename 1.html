To Upload a webpage on google for free


Wanted to learn HTML coding visit " https://www.w3schools.com/html/default.asp " and create your webpage.


Now lets start our process

Follow the following steps to upload your webpage in google...

  • Go to the website "Github.com" and sign in yourself


  • Then click the "+" icon on the right corner and select "New Repository"



  • Now give your repository name, give your description (it's optional), and select either to public or private (you can control who can see), click "A READEM FILE" and finally "click create repository"



  • Then you will go to another page then click "Add file" and then click "Upload file"


  • Either drag your files or webpage from your system or choose your files



  • After uploading scroll down, and click "Commit Changes".



  • Now once done click "Settings symbol".



  • Now scorll down the page and below source click none and select main



  • Then finally click save.
